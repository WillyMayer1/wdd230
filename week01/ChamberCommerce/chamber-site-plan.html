Chamber of Commerce of Cuyo

Objective

The purpose of the Chamber of Commerce of Cuyo is to contribute to the economic and social progress of Cuyo, defending the principles of an economy integrated into international markets. We seek to collaborate and participate in the development of export and import initiatives, promoting the actions undertaken by small and medium-sized companies from all sectors, in order to facilitate their growth and consolidation in the world.
